# Wayne Passion Reggae Kitchen — Design Philosophy

## Selected Design Approach: **Reggae Soul Maximalism**

### Design Movement
**Reggae Soul Maximalism** — A bold fusion of Caribbean energy, Rastafarian symbolism, and premium global branding. This approach celebrates vibrant color, cultural authenticity, and unapologetic boldness while maintaining luxury and sophistication.

### Core Principles

1. **Vibrant Authenticity**: The Rasta tri-color (red, gold, green) is not decorative—it's the soul of the brand. Every design decision reinforces this cultural heritage.
2. **Bold Typography & Hierarchy**: Large, commanding display fonts paired with readable body text. The brand name dominates; product names sing.
3. **Layered Depth**: Strategic use of shadows, overlays, and texture creates a premium, hand-crafted feel—not flat, not generic.
4. **Movement & Energy**: Animations reflect Caribbean rhythm: smooth, flowing, organic. Nothing rigid or corporate.

### Color Philosophy

**Primary Palette:**
- **Red** (#DC143C or similar): Passion, heat, the fire of jerk spices
- **Gold/Yellow** (#FFD700): Warmth, sunshine, Caribbean light
- **Green** (#228B22): Island life, natural ingredients, growth
- **Black** (#1A1A1A): Luxury backdrop, sophistication, contrast
- **White/Cream** (#F5F5F5): Clarity, premium feel

**Emotional Intent**: The Rasta colors evoke pride, cultural identity, and Caribbean soul. Black provides luxury and contrast. Together, they create a premium, unapologetic brand presence.

### Layout Paradigm

**Asymmetric, Flowing Sections**: Avoid rigid grids. Use diagonal cuts, organic spacing, and staggered layouts. Sections flow like Caribbean waves—some full-width, some asymmetric, some with angled dividers.

- Hero: Full-width, product image on one side, bold text on the other (asymmetric)
- About: Text flows around imagery; no centered blocks
- Products: Staggered grid with varied card sizes and hover depths
- Flavor Sommelier: Centered but with organic, flowing background elements

### Signature Elements

1. **Rasta Stripe Accents**: Thin horizontal tri-color stripes appear throughout—hero section, product cards, section dividers
2. **Palm Tree Motif**: Subtle palm silhouettes in backgrounds, section dividers, or as watermarks
3. **Angled Dividers**: SVG wave/diagonal cuts between sections create dynamic flow and break monotony

### Interaction Philosophy

**Tactile & Responsive**: Every interaction feels intentional. Hover states reveal depth (shadows lift, colors brighten). Buttons feel pressable. Cards respond to movement.

- Hover: Cards lift with shadow, colors shift toward gold/red
- Click: Subtle scale or color pulse
- Scroll: Lazy animations reveal content smoothly

### Animation Guidelines

- **Entrance**: Elements fade in and slide up gently (200–400ms)
- **Hover**: Smooth color transitions (150ms), shadow lift (200ms)
- **Scroll**: Parallax on hero image, lazy load animations on product cards
- **Rhythm**: All animations follow a consistent easing (ease-out for entrance, ease-in-out for hover)
- **Caribbean Soul**: Animations should feel organic and flowing, not mechanical

### Typography System

**Font Pairing:**
- **Display Font**: "Playfair Display" or "Bebas Neue" (bold, commanding, luxury)
  - Used for: Brand name, section headings, product names
- **Body Font**: "Poppins" or "Outfit" (modern, readable, warm)
  - Used for: Descriptions, flavor notes, body text

**Hierarchy:**
- H1 (Brand Name): 48–64px, bold, Rasta red or gold
- H2 (Section Headings): 36–48px, bold, black or dark green
- H3 (Product Names): 24–32px, semi-bold, black with gold accent
- Body: 16–18px, regular, dark gray or black
- Small Text: 14px, light, muted gray

---

## Implementation Notes

- **Color Contrast**: All text on Rasta colors uses white or black for maximum readability
- **Spacing**: Generous padding and margins create breathing room; no cramped layouts
- **Texture**: Subtle grain or noise overlay on sections adds tactile premium feel
- **Mobile First**: Asymmetric layouts adapt gracefully to mobile; staggered grids become single column
- **Accessibility**: Maintain WCAG AA contrast ratios; ensure all interactive elements are keyboard-accessible
